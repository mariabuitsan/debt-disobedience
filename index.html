debt-disobedience

This space is created for those who struggle with their debts and lose sleep at night or fall into anxious spirals. It especially embraces the feminized and racialized working-class folks, so we can –as Luci Cavallero and Veronica Gago have inspired us to do– take our debts out of the closet and fix our gaze to the historic and economic conditions that purposefully and unjustly indebted households, women, LGBTQ+ youth, trans workers, single mothers, and migrant families.
